Cloud SQL Proxy — Guia Rápido (Windows)

0. Instalar o SDK:
https://docs.cloud.google.com/sdk/docs/install-sdk
1. Download
Baixe o executável (Windows 64-bit):
https://docs.cloud.google.com/sql/docs/mysql/sql-proxy?hl=pt-br#windows-64-bit
---
2. Configurar credenciais (ADC)
Defina a variável de ambiente no PowerShell:
PowerShell
$env:GOOGLE_APPLICATION_CREDENTIALS="C:\CloudSQL\credentials.json"
Show more lines
Referências úteis:

 https://docs.cloud.google.com/docs/authentication/application-default-credentials
 https://docs.cloud.google.com/docs/authentication/provide-credentials-adc#how-to
---
3. Executar o Proxy
No mesmo diretório do executável:
PowerShell
dev → ./cloud-sql-proxy.exe --address 127.0.0.1 --port 5432 dataservices-non-prod:us-central1:posh-postgre-dev
prod → ./cloud-sql-proxy.exe --address 127.0.0.1 --port 5432 dataservices-non-prod:us-central1:posh-postgre-prod
Show more lines
---
4. Conectar
Use o cliente PostgreSQL apontando para:
Host: 127.0.0.1
Port: 5432
